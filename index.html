Guide

1. MetaMask

First of all, install web3 client like MetaMask from here and put some Ether in it.

2. Buy EBANK Tokens

To buy EBANK Token, enter amount of Ether you want to invest and simply click the "Buy Tokens" button. When confirming the transaction on MetaMask, be sure to use a high enough gas price so that the price doesn't change drastically while the transaction is in progress. Buying coins will increase both the Buy Price and Sell Price with 0.00000001 ETH per bought coin once the transaction is complete.

3. Checking Balance

Under EBANK Balance you can see how many EBANK you currently own. Note that when cashing out coins, the Buy Price and Sell Price drop afterwards, decreasing the value of your (and everyone else's) coins by 0.00000001 ETH per coin.

4. Earning Dividends

Every time EBANK are bought or sold, a percentage of the fee from the bought/sold coins will be divided under the current EBANK holders (Dividends).

5. Sell Tokens

You can move your tokens into Dividends which then is stored as Ether. To do this enter amount and click on the "Sell Tokens" button - and your tokens will be transferred into Ether based upon the current sell price.

6. ReInvest Dividend in Tokens

You can also use your dividends to immediately purchase more EBANK tokens by selecting "ReInvest Dividends".

7. Cash Out / Transfer EBANK

To cash out your EBANK, enter amount of token and click the "Withdraw" button and follow the prompts.
